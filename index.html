ABOUT ME

I Data

My greatest passion is the pursuit of knowledge. As a person, I'm a multi-lingual, jack-of-all-trades trivia buff. So, as a software engineer, I'm a data enthusiast. From simple visualizations to complex machine learning algorithms, I embrace it all. In my work, I strive to create platforms which allow everyone to take in information and experience knowledge in the most clear and accurate way possible.

My tech stack currently spans Python, SQL, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS, R, MATLAB and Swift, but I'm always working to expand my skills and to master my craft.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy cooking, baking and making my own DIY food projects. Ask me about the birthday cakes I've whipped up for friends or my latest batch of homemade catsup.
